IMPORTANT PRODUCT DIRECTIVE
DO NOT redesign, rebuild, replace or heavily modify screens that are already successful.

The current NAFA application already has a strong foundation.

Many screens already look professional and should remain mostly unchanged.

Your mission is to identify what is already working well and preserve it.

Improve only where necessary.

==================================================== PRESERVE EXISTING QUALITY
The following screens are already considered good foundations:

Home Screen
Product Cards
Product Listing
Product Detail
Orders
Profile
Bottom Navigation
Category Chips
Existing Layout Structure
Do not redesign these screens.

Do not change their navigation logic.

Do not replace working components.

Only improve readability, spacing, visual quality and content richness.

==================================================== HOME SCREEN PRIORITY CHANGE
The application should open directly on the Home Screen after the splash screen.

Users should be able to explore the marketplace immediately.

Do NOT force login before browsing.

Guest mode should be supported.

Visitors must be able to:

Browse products
Browse categories
Browse restaurants
Browse properties
Browse services
Search products
Authentication should only be required for:

Ordering
Publishing
Messaging
Favorites
Profile management
The goal is to reduce friction and increase adoption.

==================================================== MAKE CONTENT EASIER TO READ
Many users are new to technology.

Increase readability across the entire application.

Improve:

Font sizes
Button sizes
Section titles
Card spacing
Margins
Image visibility
Avoid tiny text.

Avoid small touch targets.

Important information must be readable instantly.

==================================================== LARGER IMAGES
Product images should be slightly larger.

Restaurant images should be larger.

Property images should be larger.

Service images should be larger.

Visual understanding is critical.

Users should understand products before reading.

==================================================== REMOVE EMPTY FEELING
Several screens feel unfinished because they contain too much empty space.

Do not leave large blank areas.

Add useful content blocks.

Examples:

Product Detail:

Similar products
Seller information
Delivery information
Related products
Restaurant Detail:

Popular dishes
Reviews
Similar restaurants
Restaurant statistics
Property Detail:

Similar properties
Property features
Contact owner
Profile:

Recent activity
Favorites
Saved items
==================================================== NO DEAD SCREENS
Remove all placeholder screens.

Remove all "This screen doesn't exist" pages.

Fix broken navigation.

Every navigation entry must lead to a real screen.

No dead routes.

No unfinished pages visible to users.

==================================================== CONTENT DENSITY
Every screen should feel useful.

Every screen should answer:

"What can I do next?"

Users should never feel lost.

Provide:

Recommendations
Related content
Suggested actions
without creating clutter.

==================================================== PROFESSIONAL STARTUP FEEL
The application should feel like a real business platform already operating in Guinea.

Not a demo.

Not a prototype.

Not a template.

The user must feel that NAFA is already becoming:

The agriculture marketplace of Guinea
The food ordering platform of Guinea
The real estate platform of Guinea
The services marketplace of Guinea
while keeping agriculture as the flagship activity.

==================================================== FINAL RULE
Before modifying any screen ask:

"Is the current version already good?"

If YES:

Keep it.

Only improve details.

If NO:

Improve it.

Never replace a good screen with a completely different design.

The objective is evolution, not redesign.
